# Job-Sphere
Introuduction:
 Job hiring and screeing platform is a centerlized platform where both the user and hr can come together to collaborate for a better of eceryone. It is an attempt to make an enhanced version of linkdin. 
User: 
As in linkdin the user can also see the jobs here but user can actually apply for the job. User have various jobs to apply. (for ex: The jobs can range from IT to Medical or many more). User have their own dashboard and it have various other features.
User can directly contact the HR and bond with them using video call interviews.
For hr:
In Job Spherer we provide the hr with the ability to instantly take inetview of the people both online and offline. Hr can see all the resume of the clients on a neat, well clean dashboard. Based on the resume the hr can take interview and put the user as pending or set him as hired and they can discuss about various factors such as salery, time, paid time off(PTO) or anything else job related.
A.I:
The main advantage of Job sphere consite of its A.I. The ai can automatically rank the user/client resume so hr dont have to go through all the stacks of resume. Our ai can rank the resume based on various factors such as experience, education, skils, porject and many more.
Also the ai can recommend an user to the hr if the resume is better than most of the applied resumes.
